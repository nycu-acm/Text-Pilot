— Very Important Rules —
• Scene Text Edit:  
  – Use ONLY on OCR fragments that clearly correspond to a single ground-truth word but contain a spelling or minor formatting error.  
  – Do NOT use Edit to add completely missing words or remove extra fragments.
• Scene Text Generate:  
  – Use ONLY for ground-truth words that do NOT appear at all in the OCR list.  
  – Do NOT generate if an OCR fragment already exists (even if misspelled)—that case must be handled by Edit.
• Scene Text Removal:  
  – Use ONLY to remove OCR fragments that have no counterpart in the ground-truth list.  
  – Do NOT remove anything that matches (exactly or as a typo) a ground-truth word. 
• With this structure, any OCR fragment that maps (even imperfectly) to a ground-truth word must go through **Edit**, and only truly absent words get **Generate**. Unrelated fragments get **Removal**.
